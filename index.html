RENJING XU

ASSISTANT PROFESSOR

MICROELETRONICS

THE HONGKONG UNIVERSITY OF SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY (GUANGZHOU)

Renjing Xu is an Assistant Professor at the Microelectronics Thrust, Function Hub of the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou). He received a B.Eng (First-class Honor) in photonic system from the Australian National University in 2015 and a Ph.D in applied physics from Harvard University in 2021 (advised by Prof. Donhee Ham). From 2015 to 2016, he was a visiting scholar in the Photonics Lab at University of Wisconsin - Madison.
